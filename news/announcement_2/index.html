The Asian Development Bank (ADB) and the International Economic Association (IEA) - Innovation Police Research Award 2024 acknowledge research excellence including honorable mentions of high-quality papers.

Abstract: This paper uses the 2010 flood as a natural experiment to measure the long-term impacts on human capital. Microdata from the years 2019 and 2020 indicate that cohorts in utero during the flood displayed reduced rates of functional and cognitive development, decreased educational attainment, and increased learning losses compared with other birth cohorts and unaffected children. In addition, the exposed households to the flood have lower income, lower housing conditions, and higher food insecurity compared with non-exposed households.
